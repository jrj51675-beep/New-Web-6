Water Scarcity

Water is life, yet it is running out. 2.2 billion people lack access to safe drinking water.


  • Drought: Crops failing, leading to famine and migration.
  • Conflict: Wars fought over access to rivers and aquifers.
  • Sanitation: Lack of clean water leads to preventable diseases.

Solutions

💧 Save Water

Turn off taps while brushing, fix leaks immediately, and use water-efficient appliances.

🛑 Reduce Plastic

Use reusable bags, bottles, and containers. Say no to single-use straws and cutlery.

🧠 Mental Awareness

Practice mindfulness, talk openly about feelings, and limit social media consumption.

📵 Digital Detox

Set screen time limits, have phone-free zones (like the bedroom), and engage in outdoor hobbies.
